# Knots Hitches - Hitches
Hitches attach a rope to a fixed object — a post, rail, ring, or another rope under tension — and hold only while that tension is on.

## The working hitches

- **Clove hitch** — two opposing loops over a post; fast, adjustable, slips under a varying load. Good for temporary tie-offs.
- **Round turn & two half-hitches** — a full wrap that takes the strain, finished with two half-hitches; the dependable mooring hitch.
- **Taut-line hitch** — a sliding, grip-when-loaded loop for guy-lines you want to tension by hand.
