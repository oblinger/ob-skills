# Knots Bends - Bends
Bends join two ropes into one longer line; the right bend depends on whether the ropes match in diameter and how easily you need to untie it later.

## The working bends

- **Sheet bend** — the standard join for two ropes, including unequal diameters; doubled for a more secure hold.
- **Double fisherman's** — two interlocking strangle knots; permanent, low-profile, the choice for cord and climbing slings.
- **Zeppelin bend** — symmetric and jam-resistant; unties cleanly even after heavy load.
