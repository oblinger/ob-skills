> **Canonical grouped dispatch.** A [[Collection]] with **> 15 members** → **member groups** (the [[granularity]] graduation from flat). Read the structure:
> - **Each group-row label is a link** (`[[Devtools Build|Build]]`) *down* to that group's own container page — which has **its own** dispatch table of the group's members. That's the [[progressive-disclosure]] **tree of containers**: this page is one node; each `+` row expands to a finer node.
> - **`+`** marks the label as an **expandable container** (not a leaf).
> - The members shown in each row are a *preview* hand-pinned by the author; the full list lives on the group's page.
> - The table **ends with an electric-list marker** — here `| ... |` (compact-auto staging) — so newly-added, not-yet-grouped children land there automatically until the author promotes them into a group. (`---` auto-list or trailing `+` rows are the other electric endings.)
